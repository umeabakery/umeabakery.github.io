Hi there,

So you are interested in becoming one of UB's resellers? GREAT! It's easy and straight forward.

  • KvK

    To become one of our partners / resellers, you must be registered in the Chamber of Commerce in the Netherlands (KvK ID).

  • Ordering

    There's a minimum order of 6 batches (5 bullar per batch, aka 30 bullar in total).

    Flavours available; Cinnamon, Vanilla, Cacao, Cardamom.

    • 6 batches (30 bullar) €82.50 (€ 2.75 per bulle)
    • 8 batches (40 bullar)€100.00 (€ 2.50 per bulle)
    • 10 batches (50 bullar) €120.00 (€ 2.40 per bulle)

  • Packaging

    It's one of our missions at Umeå Bakery to reduce our CO2 emission. Therefore, we do not use disposable packaging with our partners/resellers. We package our bullar in freezer containers and reuse them with every delivery. We request statiegeld (deposit) for each of the boxes.

  • Extra points

    Pssst.. We care about your mission and vision! For us, one of our missions that's close to our hearts is to become a climate-positive company.

    We care deeply about relationships and engagements to our customers and partners, so don't be shy and tell us your stories!
